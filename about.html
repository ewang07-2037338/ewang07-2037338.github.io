About Us

We're Suyue Shop, and we're run by an artist duo based in the United States! Suyue comes from our Chinese names, Suyu and Yuechen! We started Suyue Shop as a fun project way to express ourselves and to allow others to express themselves as well. We launched Suyue Shop in the Winter of 2020 as a small Instagram shop, but recently with all the support from our followers, we've moved to Etsy! In the future, we hope to be able to move to our own site too! ♡ Our aim is to spread joy to all anime fans by creating merch from all your favorite shows!
Suyu♡

I'm Suyu and one of the cofounders of Suyue Shop! I love art, music, hoarding useless things, and all things cute! I pack all keychain orders and after Yue takes photos of our products, I edit all the photos for our accounts!

Yue♡

I'm Yue and one of the cofounders of Suyue Shop! I love stationery, art, penpalling, and bullet journaling! I pack all the sticker orders and write thank you notes for all our orders! I take the photos for our accounts, while Suyu edits them!

Our Products

We currently specialize in stickers and keychains, but definitely plan on expanding in the future! All our stickers are glittery, handmade, and water resistant (though not completely waterproof). All our current keychains are 2 inches tall and extra glossy, and for our next restock, we plan on trying a holographic design! Some future products we hope to release are buttons, enamel pins, plushes, washi tapes, and sticker sheets!

Our Designs

Being students, we have to balance our business with school, so we can't churn out new designs and products every week. However, we try to work on new designs whenever we have time and when new designs are added to our shop, we'll always make an announcement on Instagram so that our followers are the first to know!
